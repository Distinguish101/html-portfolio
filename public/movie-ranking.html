My top 3 animes watched this year (2025)

To qualify I must have seen at least one full episode this year.

Blue Lock S2

The final episode was all I have been waiting for!!. Nagi & Isagi's goal, Shidou's bicycle, Rin's flow, the genius that is Sae!!

Dandadan

Very good anime. I like its quirkiness and how they make it deep sometimes. Left us on a cliffhanger though :(

Vinland Saga S1 & S2

Very beautiful animation. The character development is top tier, we literally see it happen. Crazy good fights in S1. Lovely animated scenes too.
